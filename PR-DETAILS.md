# Space Tourism Insurance Smart Contracts

## 🚀 Overview

This pull request introduces a comprehensive **parametric insurance platform** specifically designed for commercial space flights. The system leverages blockchain technology to provide transparent, automated insurance solutions for the rapidly growing space tourism industry.

## 📋 What's Included

This implementation delivers three core smart contracts that work together to create a complete insurance ecosystem:

### 🌩️ Launch Conditions Oracle (`launch-conditions-oracle.clar`)
- **Real-time weather monitoring** and environmental data integration
- **Space traffic analysis** with collision risk assessment  
- **Launch window optimization** algorithms
- **Risk scoring system** with customizable thresholds
- **Automated safety assessments** for launch decisions

**Key Features:**
- Dynamic weather condition tracking (wind, visibility, precipitation, temperature)
- Space debris and orbital traffic monitoring
- Authorized oracle network for data integrity
- Emergency override capabilities for critical situations
- Comprehensive launch site management

### 🔧 Vehicle Health Monitor (`vehicle-health-monitor.clar`)
- **Comprehensive system health tracking** for spacecraft
- **Pre-flight safety inspections** and certification
- **Real-time telemetry monitoring** during operations
- **Predictive maintenance alerts** and scheduling
- **Emergency response protocols** for critical failures

**Key Features:**
- Multi-parameter health scoring (fuel, oxygen, engine temp, battery, vibration, pressure)
- Safety inspection workflow with pass/fail tracking
- Maintenance record management with warranty tracking
- Critical system failure detection and alerting
- Authorized technician certification system

### 💰 Flight Cancellation Compensation (`flight-cancellation-compensation.clar`)
- **Automated payout processing** for flight disruptions
- **Parametric insurance policies** with instant claims
- **Risk-based premium calculation** system
- **Multi-tier coverage options** (Basic, Premium, Comprehensive)
- **Emergency compensation protocols** for critical incidents

**Key Features:**
- Instant compensation for delays and cancellations
- Flexible policy terms and coverage amounts
- Automated premium calculation based on flight risk profiles
- Claims processing workflow with authorized validators
- Insurance pool management with transparent fund tracking
